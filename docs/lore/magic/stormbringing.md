**Stormbringing / Stormwielding** is a type of magic blessing from the god [Langit](../../characters/gods/langit). Stormwielding involves manipulating storms, lightning, thunder, etc. without being able to generate them. Stormbringing involves being able to manipulate and create storms, lightning, thunders, etc. Those blessed by Langit normally start out as Stormwielders and graduate to Stormbringers after mastering more abilities.

## Abilities
Any user from Tier 0 to 2 is a Stormwielder, and any user in Tier 3 and above is a Stormbringer. 

### Tier 0 - Innate
#### Electricity Resistance
The user is extremely resistant to electricity, being nearly invulnerable to direct and indirect effects, such as muscle contractions or cardiac arrest.

### Tier 1 - Beginner
#### Rain Manipulation
The user can create, shape and manipulate rain, liquid water in the form of droplets that have condensed from atmospheric water vapor and then precipitated, i.e. become heavy enough to fall under gravity. 

#### Cloud Manipulation
The user can create, shape and manipulate clouds, visible masses of liquid droplets, various gases and/or frozen crystals produced by the condensation of water vapor that is suspended and sustained by upward-moving air currents in the atmosphere above the surface of a planetary body.

### Tier 2 - Intermediate
#### Storm Manipulation
User can negate any/all storms and storm-using powers, nullifying things such as thunderstorms and blizzards.

#### Thunder Manipulation
The user can generate, control, and manipulate thunder, wielding the sheer force of sound produced by lightning strikes or other explosive atmospheric disruptions.

#### Lightning Summoning
Users can call forth lightning from the sky that could be used to blind, stun, and/or severely burn their opponents even to the point of death they can range, from minor to fatal voltage. 

### Tier 3 - Advanced
#### Storm Manipulation
The user can manipulate storms, bending the atmosphere to their will with an almost elemental connection to the sky. 

#### Lightning Generation
Users can project lightning bolts from their own bodies (usually from owns hands), using them to pierce and/or severely burn their opponents even to the point of death.

#### Storm Generation
The user is able to generThe user can summon and shape a [perfect storm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perfect_storm)—a devastating convergence of all storm elements at their peak intensity, synchronized with flawless precision.ate various storms of wind, rain, hail, snow, sand, dust, and lightning.

### Tier 4 - Master
#### Perfect Storm
The user can summon and shape a perfect storm—a devastating convergence of all storm elements at their peak intensity, synchronized with flawless precision.
