**Shadowbringing** is the ability to create and manipulate shadows. It is a type of magic blessing from the god [Yinying](../../../characters/gods/yinying). Shadowbringing is the inverse of [Lightbringing](./lightbringing). This type of blessing is considered one of the most powerful, since it does not rely on any external elements.

[Tadhana Bakunawa](../../../characters/tadhana) is the only known Shadowbringer since the [Black and Gold Bloodbath](./) in 479 AA.

## Abilities

### Tier 0 - Innate
#### Night Vision
Able to see in low-light conditions and total darkness. This is an implicit ability for all Shadowbringers.

### Tier 1 - Beginner
#### Shadow Stealth
The user can hide within the shadows and remain undetected. They can use this technique to spy on individuals without leaving behind physical evidence. May apply to the shadows of non-living things or living organisms.

#### Shadow Generation & Manipulation
Generate and project shadows and darkness which absorb or nullifies light. There is no known case in which a person has been blessed with the ability to manipulate shadows, but not create them.

### Tier 2 - Intermediate
#### Darkness Solidification
Condense darkness/shadows into a tangible form-beginners start with a loose jelly and work their way up to metal-like hardness.

#### Personal Void
Shadowbringers can create portable void and space within or around their bodies that they can use to entrap objects or living beings. It requires minimal energy since none is exerted outside the wielder. Users can bring guests in if they touch the void from outside and user lets them in. Users can store objects, but no guests can take anything. From the user's point of view, this is a completely separate realm.

### Tier 3 - Advanced
#### Shadow Constructs
The user can make weapons, armor, shields, or even clothing of darkness/shadow.

#### Wing Manifestation
The user can use shadows to create functional wings for themselves.

### Tier 4 - Master
#### Shadow Walking
The user can essentially use shadows to teleport to another location. The farther the location, the more energy spent. Tadhana intentionally keeps this ability a secret from most people.
